# 🛡️ Emergency Shelter Instructions (Mamad)

This guide explains what to do when a rocket alarm sounds and how to safely enter a protected space (Mamad / shelter).

---

## 🚨 When the Alarm Sounds

1. Stop what you are doing immediately.
2. Move quickly and calmly to the nearest **protected space (Mamad / shelter)**.
3. Enter the shelter and **close the door securely**.

---

## 🏠 Inside the Shelter

- Stay calm.
- Sit away from the **door and windows**.
- Wait **at least 10 minutes** after the alarm ends before leaving.

---

## ⚠️ If There Is No Mamad Nearby

Go to one of the following safer locations:

- 🪜 **Internal stairwell**
- 🧱 **Interior hallway without windows**
- 🛏️ Lie on the ground and **protect your head with your hands** if no shelter is available

---

## 📢 Important

- Follow official safety instructions.
- Do not leave the protected space too early.
- Help **children, elderly people, and anyone who needs assistance**.

---

## 🎒 Recommended Emergency Kit

Keep these items inside the shelter:

- 💧 Drinking water  
- 🔦 Flashlight  
- 📱 Mobile phone + charger  
- 🩹 First aid kit  
- 📻 Radio or device for receiving updates  

---

## 🙏 Stay Safe

Remain calm and follow safety guidelines during emergencies.
